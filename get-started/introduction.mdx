- **Lead Qualification**: Automate the process of gathering and filtering leads for your business.

## Key Features

- **Deployment Options**:
  - [Embed on your website](/integrations/web/iframe).
  - Integrate with [Wordpress](/integrations/web/wordpress) or other platforms.
  
- **Real-time Data Collection**:
  - [Monitor](/submission/realtime) and analyze user interactions as they happen.
  
- **Customizable Workflows**:
  - [Design](/editor/graph) tailored chat flows using intuitive drag-and-drop tools.
  
## Getting Started

1. **Visit the Documentation**:  
   Use the search box in the Agent documentation to find detailed answers and instructions tailored to your specific use case.

2. **Create Your First Agent**:  
   Follow the [step-by-step guide](/guides/create-first-agent) to design your first conversational agent.

3. **Deploy and Monitor**:  
   [Publish](/editor/publish) your Agent and use the [analytics tools](/submission/overview) to track its performance in real-time.
