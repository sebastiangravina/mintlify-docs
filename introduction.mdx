# Release0 Documentation

Release0 is a versatile platform for building conversational applications or forms. It allows users to create, customize, and deploy interactive chat-based experiences for a variety of purposes, such as:

- **Lead Qualification**: Automate the process of gathering and filtering leads for your business.
- **Customer Support**: Provide instant assistance to your users or customers with predefined workflows.
- **Product Launch**: Engage users with interactive conversations to introduce new products or services.
- **User Onboarding**: Simplify onboarding experiences for new users.
- **AI Chats**: Create AI-driven chat experiences for various use cases.

## Key Features

- **Deployment Options**:
  - Embed on your website.
  - Integrate with WhatsApp or other platforms.
  
- **Real-time Data Collection**:
  - Monitor and analyze user interactions as they happen.
  
- **Customizable Workflows**:
  - Design tailored chat flows using intuitive drag-and-drop tools.
  
## Getting Started

1. **Visit the Documentation**:  
   Use the search box in Release0 documentation to find detailed answers and instructions tailored to your specific use case.

2. **Create Your First Agent**:  
   Follow the step-by-step guide to design a conversational flow that meets your needs.

3. **Deploy and Monitor**:  
   Publish your Agent and use the analytics tools to track its performance in real-time.
