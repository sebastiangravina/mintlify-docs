---
title: Add a Custom Domain
description: "Learn how to bind a custom domain to your Release0 agent for better accessibility."
---

You can bind a custom domain to your **agent** in the **Workspace** settings. This allows you to use your own domain name to access your agent.

For more information on how to add a custom domain to your agent plesae see the **[Domains](/workspace/customDomains)** section in the documentation.
