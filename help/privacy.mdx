We, Release0, part of MobApp Labs LLC ("Release0", "Company" or "we") value your privacy and we are committed to safeguarding your personal information and being transparent about the data we collect and how we use it.
Our Privacy Policy applies to all interactions you may have with Release0 and is intended to help you understand our practices concerning the collection, use, and disclosure of information. We retain the right to amend or update this Privacy Policy at any time. The updated version will be indicated by an updated “Last update” date, and the updated version will take effect immediately as soon as it is accessible.
Please note: It is vital to read and understand our Privacy Policy. By engaging in any form of communication with Release0, you acknowledge you have read and understood this Privacy Policy. Should you disagree with our policy, we kindly ask you not to interact with any aspect of our business.
The content of this document is greatly inspired by Plausible's Privacy Policy, which serves as a source of inspiration for transparency and privacy.
In this policy, we outline what data we collect and why, how your data is handled, and your rights regarding your data. **We promise we never sell your data.**

## As a visitor to the release0.com website

- No personal information is collected.
- No information such as cookies is stored in the browser.
- No information is shared with advertising companies.
- No information is monetized.

We use **Google Analytics** to collect anonymous data with the goal of tracking overall trends in website traffic. Data collected includes:

- Referral sources
- Top pages
- Visit duration
- Information from devices (device type, operating system, country, browser) used during the visit

## As a user of the Release0 builder

Our guiding principle is to collect only what is necessary to provide the best service to you. We use a select number of trusted external service providers for certain functionalities.

### Services We Use

1. **Cookies**:  
   A persistent first-party cookie is stored to remember your login session. By logging into your account, you consent to using cookies to avoid repeated logins during each session.  
   - You can adjust cookie retention settings in your browser.  
   - Stored cookies may be deleted at any time.

2. **Web App Hosting**:  
   We use **Vercel** for hosting the web app. Static content (HTML, CSS, JavaScript) is cached at Vercel's Edge Network, serving files from servers closest to you. Server Functions (API calls) are hosted in **London, UK (lhr1)**.  
   - See the [Vercel Privacy Policy](https://vercel.com/legal/privacy-policy) for details.

3. **Database Hosting**:  
   Our database is hosted by **Supabase** in **AWS us-east-1 (North Virginia)**.  
   - See the [Supabase Privacy Policy](https://supabase.com/privacy) for details.

4. **File Storage**:  
   Files are stored in **AWS S3** located in the **us-east-1 (North Virginia)** region.

5. **DDoS Protection and CDN**:  
   We use **Cloudflare** for DDoS protection and CDN services.  
   - See the [Cloudflare Privacy Policy](https://www.cloudflare.com/privacypolicy/) for details.

6. **Payment Processing**:  
   Payments for upgrades to paid plans are handled by **Stripe**. Billing information and the payment process are managed directly by Stripe.  
   - See the [Stripe Privacy Policy](https://stripe.com/privacy) for details.

7. **Error Tracking**:  
   Anonymous error tracking is done using **Sentry** to meet our software quality standards.  
   - See the [Sentry Privacy Policy](https://sentry.io/privacy/) for details.

8. **Email Services**:  
   Transactional emails and email reports are sent using **Amazon SES**.  
   - See the [Amazon SES Privacy Policy](https://docs.aws.amazon.com/ses/latest/dg/data-protection.html) for details.

9. **Behavioral Analytics**:  
   We use **PostHog EU** to collect anonymous behavioral data, enabling product improvement.  
   - See the [PostHog Privacy Policy](https://posthog.com/privacy) for details.

## As a user of an agent

You are bound by the agents creator’s configuration regarding data collection and handling. **We do not collect any personal data from agents users.** Additionally:

- No behavioral insights are used to sell advertisements.
- No personally identifiable information is collected, stored, or misused.

If the **Remember User** setting is enabled, data is stored in either the browser's local storage or session storage. This allows the chatbot to remember answers if a user leaves and returns later.
