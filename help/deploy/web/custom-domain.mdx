---
title: Add a Custom Domain
---

You can bind a custom domain to your **agent** in the **“Share”** tab.

### Add My Domain Button

To connect a new domain, follow these instructions:

1. Navigate to the **Share** tab in your Release0 dashboard.
2. Click on the **“Add My Domain”** button.
3. Enter your custom domain in the provided field.

### Add Domain Instructions

1. Update your DNS records as instructed in the dashboard. Typically, this involves:
   - Adding an A record or CNAME to point your domain to the specified IP or URL.
2. Once you've updated the DNS record, click the **“Save”** button.
3. Wait for a few minutes for the DNS changes to propagate.

> **Note**: DNS propagation time may vary depending on your domain provider.

## Troubleshooting

If your domain is not properly configured or verified, you will see an error icon next to your domain link.

### Error Icon

When this error icon appears:

- Click on it to view the specific steps required to fix the issue.
- Common issues include:
  - Incorrect DNS record values.
  - Missing or incomplete verification steps.

> **Tip**: Double-check the DNS settings and allow time for propagation before reattempting verification.
